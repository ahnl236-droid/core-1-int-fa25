The Art of Fragrance

Middle Notes

aka Heart Notes

The middle notes are what make the “core” of the perfume’s character, as they emerge after the top notes fade and are surfaced for a good measure of time. They bridge the gap and define the fragrance as its biggest impression of scent, with its lifetime dependent on the fragrance concentration..

Duration: 1–5 hours

Common Middle Notes: Floral, Spicy, Rich Fruits, and Lighter Woods
