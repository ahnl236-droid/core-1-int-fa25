Bottom Notes

aka The Soul

The bottom notes are the longest-lasting scents in a perfume application’s lifespan. These scents are rich and heavy, as their role following after the middle notes is to create a firm foundation of the fragrance’s remnant scent with depth and warmth.

Duration: 4–6+ hours

Common Top Notes: Vanilla, Woods, Resins, and Musk
