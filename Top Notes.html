The Art of Fragrance

Top Notes

aka The Opener

The top notes are the key impressions of your perfume application. Although they eventually fade by half an hour to bring out the middle (heart) notes, they play the important role of attracting with an initial scent that sets the tone and guides the individual through the next stages of the perfume’s formulation.

Duration: 5–20 minutes

Common Top Notes: Citrus, Light Fruits, and Fresh Herbs
